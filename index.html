The dashboard Contains



  • The network vulnerability reporting tool is expected to provide daily reports about the most recent CVE vulnerabilities released. This information will be collected based on Spotify’s inventory of network devices and software versions installed.
  • The information will be collected from the CISC, a cybersecurity initiative sponsored by the government of Luxembourg. It was “designed to provide a systematic response facility to computer security threats and incidents”.

submit a request



We in ENT would like to collaborate with other teams within PE or outisde. Hopefully this tool could become internally used and serve a purpose of providing CVE stats for all device software run in Spotify. Let us know, help? we are here!!.

how



By performing periodic cross checks of our network inventory against a CVE database. A software management life cycle process to complement the collected reports.

Purpose



  • We require visibility to detect our weak spots in the network, and specifically from the software perspective, the Common Vulnerabilities and Exposure (CVE) bulletins are a reputable source of information about it.
  • It is a great opportunity for cross-squad collaboration with 0-day, GWS, and others.
  • Visibility makes us faster and more accurate.
